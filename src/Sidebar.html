Tool Guide & Workflow
1. The Assembly Line
Paste your raw CSV into Goodreads_Import, then click:
  • Clean Data: Splices the messy CSV, separates series info, and preps the data.
  • Master Sync: Routes the clean books to your Tracker/Wishlist & updates old titles.
2. Detail Fillers
  • Sync Pages: Pulls total page counts from the database into the Reading list.
  • Sync Covers: Pastes saved Drive covers to both lists.
  • Sync Kindle: Connects to Readwise to fetch native covers.
3. Utilities & Tools
  • Ratings Calc: Toggles your CAWPILE calculator sheet open and closed.
  • Manual Routing: A direct route if Master Sync needs a bypass.
  • Scrape HTML: Extracts cover images from a saved Goodreads webpage.
  • Show/Hide Setup: Toggles visibility of your backend sheets.
