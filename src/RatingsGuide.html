✨ Pick Your Scale: Use CAWPILE for fiction/fantasy, HEARTED for your romance reads, and CRAFT for non-fiction or hobby guides. Don't forget the Spice Scale for those extra-steamy bonus points! 🌶️

⚖️ THE CAWPILE SYSTEM
Standard Fiction & Fantasy
  • Characters: Distinct voices? Clear motivations?
  • Atmosphere: Immersive world-building?
  • Writing: Engaging prose? Good pacing?
  • Plot: Compelling story? Satisfying twists?
  • Intrigue: Unputdownable?
  • Logic: Do the world rules make sense?
  • Enjoyment: Overall fun factor!

❤️ THE HEARTED SYSTEM
Romance & Romantasy Star Rating
  • Heart: Emotional stakes and depth.
  • Execution: How well the tropes were handled.
  • Attraction: Chemistry and the "spark."
  • Relationship: Growth of the couple's bond.
  • Tension: The "Slow Burn" or yearning factor.
  • Enjoyment: Overall squeal/swoon factor!
  • Denouement: Logic/Satisfaction of the HEA.

🧠 THE CRAFT SYSTEM
Non-Fiction, Memoirs & Hobby Guides
  • Clarity: Easy to follow? Good visuals?
  • Relevance: Useful or practical for your life?
  • Authority: Does the author have clear expertise?
  • Flow: Engaging to read or too dry?
  • Takeaways: Learned something new or inspired?

🔥 THE CLIMAX SCALE
Intimacy & Emotion (Out of 69)
  • ✦ Chemistry (/11): Tension and magnetic pull.
  • ✦ Lust (/11): Attraction and pure desire.
  • ✦ Intimacy (/11): Vulnerability and trust.
  • ✦ Mood (/11): Romantic vibes and atmosphere.
  • ✦ Angst (/11): Yearning and pain before payoff.
  • ✦ X-Factor (/14): Unique tropes or kinks.

🌶️ THE FLAME SCALE
Explicitness (Out of 25)
  • ✦ Foreplay (/5): The build-up and teasing.
  • ✦ Language (/5): Dirty talk and dialogue.
  • ✦ Anatomy (/5): Clinical vs. euphemistic details.
  • ✦ Mood (/5): Tone (romantic, rough, dark).
  • ✦ Explicitness (/5): On-page detail level.
